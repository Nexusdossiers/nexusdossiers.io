BREAKING: Active Shooter at Grand Blanc Church

Active Shooter Opens Fire at Grand Blanc, MI Church – Building Engulfed in Flames

Grand Blanc, Michigan • Published: Just now
Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, McCandlish Rd

At least one person is dead and more than 10 others injured after an active shooter opened fire at the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints on McCandlish Road in Grand Blanc, Michigan. The suspect—a 40-year-old male—drove a truck into the church entrance, opened fire with an assault rifle, and set the building ablaze.

Police responded within minutes and shot and killed the gunman at the scene. Authorities confirm the shooter is deceased and no longer a threat.

According to officials at a press conference, hundreds of people were inside the church when the attack began. Rescue crews are still searching the burning structure, and police expect to find additional victims.

“This was a targeted attack on Christians,” said former President Donald Trump in a statement. “The FBI is leading the federal investigation. We must end this epidemic of violence and pray for the victims and their families.”

The FBI has taken the lead on the federal investigation, working alongside Michigan State Police and local law enforcement. The motive remains under investigation.

Firefighters are battling intense flames as the church continues to burn. Emergency medical teams have transported the wounded to multiple area hospitals. Conditions of the injured are not yet known.

This is a rapidly developing story. Officials urge the public to avoid the McCandlish Road area and to refrain from sharing unverified information. Updates will be provided as they become available from official sources.
